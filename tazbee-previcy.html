Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: March 27, 2026

At Smart Tasbeeh Counter, we believe in complete privacy and transparency. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our digital tasbeeh app.

✨ Key Point: We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information. All your data stays on your device.

📊 Information We Collect

ZERO DATA We collect absolutely NO personal information.

Smart Tasbeeh Counter does not require you to create an account, provide an email, or share any personal details. The app works completely offline.

What we DON'T collect:

  • ❌ No email addresses or phone numbers
  • ❌ No location data or GPS tracking
  • ❌ No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • ❌ No usage analytics or crash reports
  • ❌ No contacts, photos, or files

💾 Data Storage

All your tasbeeh sessions, counter history, and app settings are stored exclusively on your device using a local database (Hive).

  • ✅ Data never leaves your device
  • ✅ No cloud sync or backup to external servers
  • ✅ You have full control to delete your data anytime
  • ✅ Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
🔒 Your data, your control: You can clear all sessions from the Settings screen at any time.

🔐 Permissions

Smart Tasbeeh Counter requests minimal permissions for a better user experience:

📳 Vibration Permission

Purpose: Provides haptic feedback when you tap the counter button.

Optional: You can disable this feature anytime in Settings → Enable Vibration.

Data Access: No data is collected or shared through this permission.

🌐 Third-Party Services

We use NO third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

  • ✅ No Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics
  • ✅ No Facebook SDK or other trackers
  • ✅ No ad networks or remarketing services
  • ✅ No crash reporting tools

The app is 100% independent and runs offline without connecting to any external servers.

👶 Children's Privacy

Smart Tasbeeh Counter is suitable for users of all ages. Since we collect no personal information, there is no risk to children's privacy.

  • ✅ No age restrictions or verification required
  • ✅ No targeted content or advertisements
  • ✅ No personal data collection from anyone, including children under 13

Parents can rest assured that the app is safe for children to use independently.

🛡️ Data Security

While we don't collect any data from you, we still take security seriously:

  • ✅ All local data is stored in secure device storage
  • ✅ No internet connection required, eliminating online risks
  • ✅ App follows Android and iOS security best practices
  • ✅ Regular updates to ensure compatibility and security

📋 Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, many standard privacy rights are inherently satisfied:

  • ✅ Right to Delete: Clear all sessions from Settings screen
  • ✅ Right to Control: Manage vibration, reset confirmation, and default zikr in Settings
  • ✅ Right to Uninstall: Removing the app deletes all local data
  • ✅ Right to Transparency: This policy clearly explains our practices

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally to reflect improvements or clarify our practices. Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Since we don't collect your email, we cannot notify you individually, but all updates will be clearly shown here.
